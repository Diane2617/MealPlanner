Welcome to the Meal Planner!

I can take the headache out of deciding what to cook. You may choose how you want me to generate your meal.

  • Standard Meals - these meals consist of a protein, two sides and a vegetable
  • Different Meal Arrangement - you choose how many sides and vegetables you wish to include in your meal
  • Your Build - input your items of choice for each of the different categories and the structure of your meals. Meals will then be generated based on this
So go ahead, choose the meal generator of your choice in the navigation bar.
